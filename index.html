Step-by-Step Installation & Activation

Follow these simple steps to get ReedPOS running on your system

1

Download the Installer

Click the link below to download the ReedPOS setup file directly from GitHub.

Download ReedPOS_Setup.exe

This will trigger a single-click download of the installation file.

2

Run the Installer

After download completes, locate the file and run ReedPOS_Setup.exe.

Follow the on-screen installation prompts

Choose your installation directory

Complete the installation process

3

Setup Admin Account

When ReedPOS opens for the first time, you'll see the setup window.

Enter details for your admin account

Fill in your company details

Configure your printer settings

Note: You cannot proceed with empty fields

4

Proceed to License Purchase

After filling all fields, click Proceed to continue.

Login with your newly created admin account

Navigate to the license purchase section

Select the single-user license for $199

5

Complete Payment

You will be redirected to your browser to complete the secure payment.

Complete the payment process in your browser

Return to ReedPOS application after payment

Click Check Payment in the app

6

Activate Your License

If payment was successful, you'll receive your license key.

License key will be auto-filled in the textbox

Key is also saved in Documents/ReedPOS folder

Click Activate to enable offline verification

You're ready to run your business with ReedPOS!
